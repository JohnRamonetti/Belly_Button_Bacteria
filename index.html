Biology and Beef?
Can microorganisms really produce top-quality meat?

Are we alone? The answer is a firm "No!". We share our space, our homes, even our bodies with countless microorganisms. Some are good and some are bad.

Well, now it's time to see what else some of our omnipresent organisms can do for us. In this case, we think that we can produce some top-quality beef using certain bacteria cultivated from the human navel. That's right - your belly button!

Have a look at our interactive charts and see which bacteria call your belly button home. Simply select your number from the "Test Subject ID No." drop-down menu below and see what you find!
